About Me

Hello! My name is Danielle Ruth Skinner and I'm a graduate research assistant in the Computational Cosmology Group led by Dr. John Wise at the Georgia Institute of Technology. I am interested in galaxy evolution and formation, the first stars in the universe, and matching observations with cosmological simulations.

Before joining Georgia Tech, I received by Bachelor of Science from the University of Washington with a double major in physics and astronomy and a double minor in mathematics and philosophy.
